Privacy Policy — Reactor Panic

Last updated: March 2026

Reactor Panic is a mobile game built in Godot. This policy explains what data the game collects and how it is used. Our website: https://www.aidoo.biz.

Data we collect

The game uses a developer-hosted leaderboard service. The data sent to the leaderboard is:

  • Persistent anonymous player ID
  • Optional display name (user-editable)
  • Score
  • Run duration
  • Difficulty
  • Timestamps for leaderboard entries

Data we do not collect

We do not collect location, contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or payment data. There are no ads and no third-party analytics SDKs.

How we use data

We use the data above to operate leaderboards, display rankings, and keep scores tied to a consistent anonymous player ID.

Storage and security

Leaderboard data is stored on servers hosted and managed by the developer. We use the data only for the leaderboard service and do not share it with third parties.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

Data retention

Leaderboard entries are kept for as long as the leaderboard service is available. You can request deletion at any time using the contact details below.

Your control

You can change or clear your display name at any time in the game. If you want your leaderboard data removed, contact us and we will delete it.

Children's privacy

Reactor Panic is a general-audience game and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will remove it.
